About Us

We are FairyTalez. We love fairy tales – no surprise – that is why we dedicated a our website, two apps, and several books to these wonderful tales! Fairytalez.com is now the largest collection of fairy tales, folk tales, and fables online. We are constantly looking for classic stories to add to our library, so send us a note if you know a good fairy tale we are missing. We scour the archives for great tales and share them not only here..
